Location of Manikarnika Ghat

Address

Manikarnika Ghat, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India

Directions

Manikarnika Ghat is located on the banks of the Ganges River in Varanasi. It is easily accessible by road and boat. Here are some common starting points:

  • From Varanasi Junction: Taxi/Auto (approx. 4 km)
  • From Airport: Taxi (approx. 25 km)
  • By Boat: From Dashashwamedh Ghat to Manikarnika Ghat

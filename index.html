About Manikarnika Ghat

Manikarnika Ghat is one of the oldest and most sacred ghats along the Ganges in Varanasi. Deeply connected to the cycle of life and death, this ghat is believed to offer moksha (liberation) to souls who are cremated here. The ever-burning flame symbolizes the eternal nature of the soul.
